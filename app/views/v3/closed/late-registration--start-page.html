Register late for a national professional qualification (NPQ)

You’ve been given access to make a late registration for a NPQ whilst the service is closed.

You will also need to apply separately with your training provider.

Before you start

You should have already chosen which NPQ you want to do and who your provider is. If you have not chosen yet, find out about NPQ courses.

You also need a teacher reference number (TRN) to register for a NPQ, even if you’re not a teacher. Learn about TRNs including how to request one if you do not have one.

If you work in early years or childcare, check if your workplace is registered with Ofsted and get their Ofsted unique reference number (URN) if they have one.

Before registering for an NPQ you’ll be asked to create a DfE Identity account or sign in to your account.
