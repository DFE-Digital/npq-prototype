Course modules

Module 1: Principles of inclusive teaching

This module covers:

  • Understanding the foundational principles of adaptive teaching
  • Understand the principles of an inclusive environment
  • Exploring the key components of responsive adaptive teaching, including real-time adjustments, flexible grouping, varying scaffolding techniques
  • Examining the importance of adaptive teaching in ensuring all pupils can meet expectations and thrive in an inclusive learning environment

Module 2: Identifying pupils needs

This module covers:

  • Recognise how prior attainment, social and emotional development, language and communication proficiency may impact learning
  • Understanding common misconceptions about individual pupils' needs and how to address these
  • Explore how to implement systematic approaches to build a clear picture of individual pupil needs, including their different levels of prior knowledge, and potential barriers to learning

Module 3: Applying inclusive teaching strategies in practice

This module covers:

  • Understanding the different types of cognitive load, and how they can be managed through effective lesson design and instructional strategies
  • Applying different strategies in practice
  • Making use of specialist technology to support learners

Module 4: Working with others to create an inclusive environment

This module covers:

  • Understanding the shared responsibility of inclusion across the school community
  • Collaborating effectively with colleagues, including SEND specialists, teaching assistants and parents
  • Contributing to an inclusive school culture

Module 5: Evaluating and adapting strategies

This module covers:

  • Understanding how to evaluate the effectiveness of inclusive strategies in meeting diverse pupil needs
  • Using assessments and feedback to evaluate impact
  • Developing strategies to sustain an inclusive classroom

Optional modules

Optional module: Technology / AI

This module covers:

  • Understand emerging trends in technology and AI in Education
  • Understand the role of technology in meeting diverse pupils needs
  • Reflect on ethical considerations, including data privacy and bias in AI tools

Optional module: Belonging / culture in the classroom

This module covers:

  • Understand how to embed practices to support mental health and emotional resilience
  • Understand emerging research in wellbeing and belonging in the classroom, and how it can be applied in practice

Optional module: Supporting transitions and change

This module covers:

  • Understand principles of child development
  • Understand how to implement effective inclusive strategies to support pupils through key transitions (e.g. phases, settings, year groups)
  • Understand how to lead change initatives across school systems
