Inclusive leadership fundamentals with coaching support

Learn or revisit the principles of inclusive and adaptive teaching and how to apply them when supporting your colleagues to meet the learning needs of pupils in their class and across your school. This course is followed by a series of coaching to reflect on your learning and work on implementation techniques.

Course modules

Module 1: Principles of inclusive teaching

This module covers:

  • Understanding inclusion as a proactive design princple for school planning
  • Understand how prior attainment, social and emotional development, language and communication proficiency and social and emotional development may impact learning
  • Reflect on how inclusive design benefits the whole school community

Module 2: Applying inclusive leadership in practice

This module covers:

  • Understanding the role of leadership in embedding inclusive practices across the school
  • Supporting staff to apply inclusive teaching in practice
  • Assessing your school's inclusivity and identify key areas for development

Module 3: Understanding the impact of inclusive and statutory requirements on your school

This module covers:

  • Understand key statutory requirements related to inclusion, such as the Equality Act and SEND code of practice
  • Understand how practical considerations for securing support for higher needs
  • Reflect on how inclusive design benefits the whole school community

Module 4: Working with others to create an inclusive environment

This module covers:

  • Building strong partnerships with staff, families and inclusion specialists
  • Leveraging the expertise of SEND specialists
  • Promoting inclusive practices in your school

Module 5: Creating an inclusive whole school plan

This module covers:

  • Develop a strategic vision for inclusion aligned with your whole school plan
  • Setting measurable goals and actions to embed inclusive practices
  • Creating a sustainable inclusive environment through leadership, training and resource planning
