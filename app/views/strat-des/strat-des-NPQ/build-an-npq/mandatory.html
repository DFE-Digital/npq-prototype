Mandatory modules

The full Senior Leadership NPQ course takes 18 months to complete. If you have previously completed an NPQ this will be reduced.

Part of your course is mandatory modules. These are:

  • the latest evidence-based learning and approaches to teaching and leadership
  • school culture
  • effective teaching
  • supporting curriculum and assessment development
  • behaviour
  • professional development
  • additional and special educational needs
  • organisational management

As you have previously completed the Behaviour and Culture NPQ you won't need to complete these modules again:

  • professional development
  • the latest evidence-based learning and approaches to teaching and leadership

For the rest of your course you will choose from a selection of additional modules.
